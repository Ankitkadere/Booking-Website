Information

Welcome to RoomBooking, your go-to platform for finding and booking rooms easily. We connect room owners with potential tenants, making the process seamless and efficient. Below you will find detailed information about our services, privacy policies, and terms & conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room?

To book a room, simply browse through our available listings, select the room you are interested in, and contact the room owner directly to arrange the booking and payment.

Do I need to create an account to book a room?

No, you do not need to create an account to book a room. However, room owners need to create an account to list their rooms and manage bookings.

How do I pay for the room?

All payments are to be made directly to the room owner in cash. We do not handle any payments on behalf of the room owners.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by room owner. Please review the cancellation policy of the room you are booking before making a reservation.

Can I list my room on your platform?

Yes, room owners can create an account and list their rooms on our platform. Simply sign up, provide the necessary details about your room, and start receiving bookings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee for listing my room?

No, there is no fee for listing your room on our platform. We provide this service free of charge to room owners.

How do I update my room listing?

Room owners can update their room listings by logging into their account and making the necessary changes to their listing details.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with my booking?

If you encounter any issues with your booking, please contact the room owner directly to resolve the matter. If you need further assistance, you can reach out to our customer support team.

How do I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can contact our customer support team through the contact form on our website or by emailing kaderesofttech@gmail.com
